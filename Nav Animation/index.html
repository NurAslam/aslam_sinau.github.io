Intro

Muh Nur Aslam published a Progress Nav concept on Codepen back in 2017. As pointed out by Chris in a recent blog post over at CSS Tricks, we now have the Intersection Observer API which saves us a good amount of hassle. Here's my attempt at an intersectionObserver version of Muh Nur Aslam
